Neon Genesis Evangelion

Synopsis:

Shinji Ikari, a student find himself recruited to pilot a giant biomechanical robot named a EVA by his father, Gendo. He along with Rei and Asuka are piloting the EVA to figth against alien-like entities called Angels from attack Tokyo-3.

Characters

Shinji Ikari

Shinji Ikari is the main characters, the Third Child, the designated pilot of Evangelion Unit-01, and the only son of NERV Commander Gendo Rokubungi and Gehrin bioengineer Yui Ikari. Following the early death of his mother, who disappeared in a mysterious accident, his father entrusted Shinji to a guardian. Once Shinji reaches the age of fourteen, Gendo calls him back to the city Tokyo-3 to assume the role of Third Child, a pilot assigned to command the Eva-01. Because of his abandonment, Shinji feels unwanted and, according to Hideaki Anno, "abandoned the attempt to understand himself", convincing himself he is a useless individual. Anno also described him as shy of human contact and unable to commit suicide. Compared to the stereotypical hero of a traditional mecha anime, he is devoid of energy, closed towards others, and has a pessimistic attitude. After moving to Tokyo-3, Shinji begins to form relationships that influence him positively, especially the other pilots, his classmates, and other members of the Nerv such as Misato Katsuragi, his superior and legal guardian, and Ryoji Kaji. He is voiced by Megumi Ogata in the original Japanese dub while Spike Spencer voice Shinj in the ADV English Dub and Casey Mongillo voice Shinji in the Netflix English Dub.

Asuka Langley Soryu

Asuka Langley Soryu is one of the main female characters, the Second Child, the designated pilot of Evangelion Unit-02, and the daughter of Kyoko Zeppelin Soryu. She is a red-haired, blue-eyed, Japanese-German girl who was raised and educated in Germany. Asuka is a child prodigy who graduated from college at a young age; she is brave, positive, and enthusiastic about her role as a pilot but has a pronounced inferiority complex towards males. With recent acquaintances, Asuka appears to be authoritarian. Although she has a natural spontaneity, she suffers from mental problems, which alongside her excessive self-love make her lose the ability to pilot her Evangelion in the second half of the series. Asuka is attracted to her colleague Shinji Ikari, but as events progress she starts to develop ambivalent feelings towards him. In the final episodes of the anime, Asuka develops a deep self-loathing and begins to feel anxiety at the idea of being alone. Rei Ayanami

Rei Ayanami is one of the main female characters, the First Child, the designated pilot of Evangelion Unit-00, and her date of birth us unknown. Rei is a taciturn. shy girl who mechanically carries out any order from her superiors, even if it is particularly cruel. She is committed to fighting against the Angels as though the fight is the only point of connection between her and other people, making it her only reason for living. Rei minimizes contact with others; the only exception being Gendo Ikari, to whom she is more open. As the show progresses, Rei slowly makes friends with Shinji, her colleague at Nerv and classmate, discovering human emotions and acquiring greater self-awareness. In the concluding events of the storyline, the audience learns that Rei's mysterious origins tie into the story's resolution. She is voiced by Megumi Hayashibara in the original Japanese dub while Amanda Winn-Lee voice Rei in the ADV English Dub, Brina Palencia voice Rei in Funimation Dub, and Ryan Bartley voice Rei in the Netflix English Dub.

Misato Katsuragi

Misato Katsuragi is one of the main female characters, the captain of the Nerv Operations Department, and Shinji's guardian. Due to a series of childhood emotional traumas, she has developed a frivolous, exuberant personality, displays extroversion, and leads a sloppy, messy lifestyle. Misato's affectionate personality sometimes causes her to prioritize her human feelings over her duty as a Nerv employee, and her job provokes internal conflict and causes her to make difficult choices. She then later takes in Asuka Langley Soryu. As the series progresses, through her former lover Ryoji Kaji, Misato learns the truth behind the Human Instrumentality Project and the depths of deception that NERV and SEELE have gone to keep the Project secret, even from her. She is voiced by Kotono Mitsuishi in the original Japanese dub while Allision Keith voice Misato in the ADV English Dub and Carrie Keranen voice Misato in the Netflix English Dub.

Kaworu Nagisa

Kaworu Nagisa is the Fifth Child, a major character, and the replacement pilot of Evangelion Unit-02, who is sent to Nerv as a replacement pilot for Unit 02 following Asuka's psychological breakdown. Kaworu befriends Shinji Ikari, towards whom he has a sincere and unconditional affection, and then reveals himself as the seventeenth and final Angel Tabris. Kaworu penetrates Nerv headquarters in an attempt to reunite with the first Angel Adam, but after discovering the second Angel Lilith is locked in the Terminal Dogma he asks Shinji to kill him. Kaworu was born on September 13, 2000, the same day as Second Impact. He is voiced by Akira Ishida in the Japanese dub while Kyle Sturdivant voice Kaworu in the ADV English Dub, Jerry Jewell voice Kaworu in the Funimation English Dub, and Clifford Chapin voice Kaworu in the Netflix English Dub
